style my name

a name usage notes generator disguised as a game

Coming soon: stylemy.name, an interactive game where an Archivist of Names asks you how to record your name into their stack.

At the end, the Archivist says, “This is what you’ve told me about your name:” and outputs all the text you typed in, then asks, “Is that correct?”

From there, you will be able to revise answers and regenerate the page, or say “Yes” and download your response, which will be available in txt, html, pdf, epub, rtf, and docx formats. You can post your response to your site or save it for your own reference.
